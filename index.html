About Me

I am Neda Sinehsepehr, an artist specializing in watercolor and calligraphy, based in Tehran, Iran. Since 1995, I have pursued my passion for creating art that evokes a sense of peace, tranquility, and inspiration. My work blends the subtle nuances of color with the flow of calligraphic forms, often drawing on the rich cultural heritage of Persian art. Whether through vibrant portraits or delicate watercolors, my aim is to invite viewers into a space of calm reflection, encouraging them to explore the balance between form, emotion, and nature.
