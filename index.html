About Me

I am Neda Sinehsepehr, an artist specializing in watercolor and calligraphy. My art explores the balance between form, emotion, and nature, inviting viewers into a space of peace, reflection, and contemplation. With each stroke and letter, I aim to evoke the harmonious connection between human experience and the natural world, blending traditional Persian calligraphy with modern visual elements.
